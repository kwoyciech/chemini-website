Technology: Advanced Nanoparticle Dispersions

At Chemini, we are pioneering the next generation of materials for high-performance optoelectronic devices. Our core technology revolutionizes the use of metal oxide nanoparticles by overcoming long-standing challenges of stability and processability.

Learn More

The Challenge

Conventional metal oxide nanoparticles disperse only in highly basic aqueous solutions. This limitation creates serious obstacles for their practical use:

  • •Stability Issues

    Particles aggregate and settle over time

  • •Solvent Compatibility

    Work only in strongly alkaline environments

  • •Integration Barriers

    Hard to combine with advanced materials and device architectures

The Chemini Solution: Surface Functionalization

Our breakthrough technology directly addresses these limitations through a proprietary nanoparticle surface functionalization process.

Customized Ligands

We utilize specially designed ligands that chemically bond to the nanoparticle surface. This modification fundamentally changes the particle's interaction with its environment.

Superior Stability

This process delivers highly stable nanoparticle dispersions across a broad range of solvents, from polar to non-polar, effectively overcoming the limitations of conventional systems.

Enhanced Integration

Stable dispersions make it easy to add functional ingredients, enabling precise tuning of device performance and smooth integration into complex architectures.

Applications and Impact

Perovskite Solar Cells

Next-Generation Optoelectronic Devices

This core technology directly enables the advancement of next-generation optoelectronic devices. Our primary focus is on perovskite solar cells, where the stability and quality of the nanoparticle layers are paramount for achieving high efficiency and long operational lifetimes.

  • •Compatible with n-i-p architectures
  • •Compatible with p-i-n architectures
  • •Versatile solution for research and manufacturing

Revolutionary Impact

By solving the fundamental problem of nanoparticle dispersion, Chemini is paving the way for more robust, efficient, and commercially viable electronic devices.
