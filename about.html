About Us

Pioneering Materials for the Next Generation of Technology

Our Mission

Chemini is an innovation-driven company specializing in advanced materials that power the future of electronics and renewable energy. Our mission is to accelerate R&D and industrial progress by providing the highest-purity, most reliable chemical compounds, bridging the gap between discovery and commercial application.

Our Story & Expertise

Founded in Poland in the summer of 2025, Chemini was established by senior scientists with extensive, hands-on experience in perovskite photovoltaics. This deep scientific foundation provides us with a unique understanding of the intricate material needs and challenges within this demanding technology. We don't just supply chemicals; we are experts in the advanced synthetic chemistry that enables next-generation performance.

Our Unique Advantage

Our core strength lies in our proprietary methods for the synthesis and surface modification of metal oxide nanoparticles. This exclusive expertise allows us to precisely tailor the chemistry of these advanced materials, modifying their properties to meet a broad range of specific customer needs and application requirements.

Our Values
